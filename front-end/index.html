Our Destination

Cangar

The journey began, KC's first travel in 14 August 2022. KC went to Cangar as their first destination. in cangar they took some photos and they went to a stall to eat breakfast.

Teluk Asmara Beach

KC's second travel in 22 August 2022. KC went to Teluk Asmara Beach as their second destination. on the way to teluk asmara beach, they strayed onto the sendang biru beach.

Mondangan Beach

KC's third travel in 31 January 2023. KC went to Mondangan Beach as their third destination. Little did they know, the road to Mondangan Beach is not ready yet. So they went to the paralayang near that area instead
